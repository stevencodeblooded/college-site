Why Give?

Your support helps remove financial and emotional barriers for adult learners returning to school. Every dollar goes directly toward free college admissions counseling, outreach, workshops, and student mentorship.

Signature Initiative: The Last Mile Scholarship

"Closing the gap between near-completion and graduation." This fund supports adult learners who are just a few classes away from finishing, but have exhausted their federal Pell Grant eligibility. With your help, they can graduate debt-free.
